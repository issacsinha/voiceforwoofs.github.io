Why you should adopt a dog loitering in the streets?

Why should you adopt a dog loitering in the street when you can buy various breeds from the pet shop?

There is a fundamental difference between buying a dog and adopting one, when you are buying a dog you are actually weighing it against some amount of money which is nothing compared to the unconditional love, loyalty and companionship which he has to offer for his whole life, and this is not a thing a true dog lover would do. So, when you are planning to bring a dog and if you are a true dog lover, the very thought of buying a dog should never cross your mind as the thought itself is immoral.

Now, if you are planning to adopt a dog, why not adopt a furry friend living around your house who wags his tail on seeing you for you might have fed him a couple of biscuits once or twice or you feed him regularly, as he is already a lot familiar with you and will prove to be a good pet no doubt once you adopt him. He will not only be loving, playful, loyal,protective like the other pedigree(purely breeded) dogs infact he will be better as he will be more smart, less picky about food, he will be adaptable to living conditions easily and will require less maintainence as he will be resistant to a lot of diseases. He may not mean the world to you but for him you will always be his whole world. It is a sad thing to know that the condition of stray dogs who have been abandoned or who were born there, is pathetic they don’t get proper nutrition have to search in trash for some food, have no acess to medical facilities when they are sick or hurt and have to live in drastic climatic conditions on the road, not to forget about the human torture that they are subjected to, a lot of them die facing a hard life on the streets or even in cruel roadside accidents.

So, this way you will not only be getting yourself a furry best friend for your life but you will also be contributing to a good social cause by uplifting the condition of stray dogs and by breaking the stereotype and setting an example for the people around you which in turn may also encourage them to adopt a stray.
