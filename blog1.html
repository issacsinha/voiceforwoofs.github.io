Why you should adopt a puppy from a shelter?

There's a reason dogs are known as man's best friend, they're adorable, loyal, and simply the best. But instead of visiting your local pet store, there are a lot of reasons you should hit up a shelter in honor of Adopt a Shelter Dog.

According to the ASPCA, approximately 670,000 dogs are euthanized every single year. You can save a dog's life (or two!) by choosing to adopt rather than buying your pet from a breeder.

Shelter and rescue animals are in need of a second chance. They are lost, given up or abandoned. They are unwanted and helpless. You give them a new life in a loving home and at the same time, you make room at the shelter for another pet in need of a second chance.Just because many shelter dogs were raised on street or in abusive families doesn't mean they will love you any less, shelter dogs go through socialization training to learn how to interact with people and other animal and how to remain calm in stressful situations .

When you buy a pet, you not only deny a homeless pet a home, you are supporting an industry that thrives on short-changing the welfare of animals. There are not enough homes for all the animals that are born every year. More than 8 million animals are euthanized each year. Shelter and rescue pets are spayed and neutered,and you help to break the cycle of overpopulation. Every community requires an animal shelter and rescue organizations. When you adopt a pet from a shelter or rescue you assist a not-for-profit organization and will send a message to others who will be asking you for years to come where you obtained your adorable pet.

As the old saying says ,dogs are man's bestfriend you will love your shelter pet as one of your own and they will happily return the favor.
